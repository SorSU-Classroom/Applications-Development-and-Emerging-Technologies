## Welcome to CS-321 Applications Development and Emerging Technologies!

Welcome to **CS-321 Applications Development and Emerging Technologies**!
This course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding
of applications development and emerging technologies. In this course,
you will learn about various aspects of applications development,
including web development, mobile development, cloud development, and more.
